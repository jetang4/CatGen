Welcome to CATGEN

We are a group in the CSC698 class at SFSU and we are recreating an existing program, Virtual Genetics Lab to make learning Genetics more fun and easy. We are targetting students in High School and College to use our tool to help assist their learning. We are aiming to create a tutoring tool, teaching function, and a calculator program to calculate genetic probabilities.

About Us

This project is a part of the class CSC698. We are all members of the PINC program at SFSU and we all have different levels of coding experience. This project started out as an idea of recreating a current program known as the Genetics Fly Lab. We wanted to create a student learning tool to teach students about genetics in a visually appealing and easily accessible webpage with cats!
