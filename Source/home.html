Welcome to CATGEN

We are a group in the CSC698 class at SFSU and we are recreating an existing program, Virtual Genetics Lab to make learning Genetics more fun and easy. We are targetting students in High School and College to use our tool to help assist their learning. We are aiming to create a tutoring tool, teaching function, and a calculator program to calculate genetic probabilities.
